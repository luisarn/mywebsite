Drag to Read - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Drag to Read ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is processed.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

Drag to Read is designed to operate entirely on your local device without collecting, transmitting, or storing your personal data on any external servers.

Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect personal information or user identifiers
  • Collect, store, or transmit screen capture images
  • Collect, store, or transmit text extracted from your screen
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Sell or share any data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

How the App Processes Your Data

All processing occurs locally on your device in real-time:

  • Screen captures: Captured from your screen and held in memory only — immediately discarded after text is recognized
  • Recognized text: Processed by your device's built-in text recognition — immediately discarded after being read aloud
  • Spoken audio: Generated by your device's built-in speech features — played once and not stored
  • User preferences: Saved to a local settings file on your computer

Important: No screen content, images, or extracted text ever leaves your computer.

Local Data Storage

The App stores only your personal preferences in a settings file located in your computer's application data folder. This file contains no personal identifiers and can be deleted at any time.

Stored preferences include:

  • Speech volume level
  • Speech rate/speed
  • Selected voice name
  • Language preferences (if applicable)

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

  • Screen Capture: To capture selected screen areas for text recognition
  • Text-to-Speech: To read recognized text aloud using your system voices
  • Keyboard Shortcut: To register a system-wide shortcut for quick access

Third-Party Services

The App uses built-in features of the Windows operating system to recognize text and read it aloud. These features operate entirely on your device and do not transmit data to Microsoft or any other party when used by this App.

Microsoft Store Purchases

If you purchase the App through the Microsoft Store:

  • All payment processing is handled securely by Microsoft
  • We do not have access to your payment card information, billing address, or transaction details
  • License validation is performed through Microsoft's secure servers

For more information, please visit: Microsoft Privacy Policy

Data Security

Because all processing occurs locally and no data is transmitted over the internet, your data remains under your control at all times. The App does not maintain any external databases or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

Drag to Read is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App and you have concerns, please contact us.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, correct, delete, or export. Your data remains on your local device, and you maintain full control over it.

If you wish to remove all App data:

  1. Uninstall the App
  2. Delete the settings file from your application data folder

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
