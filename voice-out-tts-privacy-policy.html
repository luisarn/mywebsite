Voice Out TTS - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Voice Out TTS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use our text-to-speech service.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Voice Out TTS operates entirely on your device. Your text inputs, voice settings, and usage patterns remain on your phone and are never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain device information and identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Information that may be collected by AdMob includes:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Advertising ID (which you can reset in your device settings)
  • IP address
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Permissions

Voice Out TTS may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access: Required solely for loading advertisements through AdMob
  • Text-to-speech engine access: To convert your text into speech using your device's built-in TTS capabilities

We do not access your microphone, contacts, camera, or storage.

Children's Privacy

Voice Out TTS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
